Getting Started

Creating Your First Dataset

  1. Click on "Create Dataset" in the dashboard
  2. Enter a description of the data you want to generate
  3. Specify the columns you need in your dataset
  4. Set the number of rows you want to generate
  5. Click "Generate Sample" and wait for the process to complete

Managing Datasets

Once generated, you can view, download, or delete your datasets from the dashboard. Each dataset shows its status, size, and creation date for easy reference.

Data Privacy

Your data is stored securely and privately. We never share your datasets with third parties. For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my data secured?

All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can download your datasets in JSON format at any time from the dashboard.

Is there a limit to dataset size?

Free accounts can generate datasets up to 10,000 rows. Contact support for larger datasets.
